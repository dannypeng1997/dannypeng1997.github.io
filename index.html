Miyazaki Hayao

Miyazaki Hayao  ( born January 5, 1941, Tokyo, Japan ) is an influential Japanese anime director whose lyrical and allusive works have won both critical ＆ popular acclaim. His notable films include Princess Mononoke (1997),Spirited Away (2001), Howl’s Moving Castle (2004), and The Boy and the Heron (2023).

Takahata Isao
